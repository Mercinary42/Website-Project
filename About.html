About Me

David Morgan was born and raised in Los Angeles, California where he graduated from Granada Hills High School. In 2003, at the age of 17, he enlisted in the United States Army as an Infantryman. During his time in service, he served four (4) deployements to Afghanistan and Iraq for a total of fourty-four (44) months.

In 2015, David Morgan was medically discharged from the Army and took up residence in Dallas, Texas. Here, he enrolled in college with a major focusing on Computer Systems and Inforamtion Technology for Businesses.

In 2017, he moved to Pennsylvania where he worked as a Welder's Helper on the Atlantic Sunrise Pipeline.

While working in Pennsylvania, David met a beautiful woman by the name of Gretchen, and they are expecting their first child in April 2019.
