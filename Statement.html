Work Ethicmore_vert
The principle that hard work is intrinsically virtuous or worthy of reward
Work Ethicclose

Working hard has been a mantra in my life for some time now. I believe that if you start a job, you must finish that job. Not just finishing the job, however, making sure that job isn't completed half-ass'd. You should be passionate about what you do, and give a 100% no matter what.

Philosophymore_vert
A theory or attitude held by a person or organization that acts as a guiding principle for behavior
Philosophyclose

You lead by doing. You set the example for others to follow. There's a creed that we follow once we complete Ranger School, and one of the stanzas that sticks with me goes as follows:

Gallanty will I show the world that I am a specially selected and well-trained Solier. My courtesy to superior officers, neatness of dress and care of equipment shall set the example for others to follow.


My philosophy is to always be first, always doing your best, and leading from the front.
